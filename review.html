The Entertainer by No Such Animal

8.5/10

No Such Animal performs at Velour's Festival of Fools in 2023 (PHOTO: No Such Animal)

No Such Animal, the rising alt-rock band from Provo, Utah, has made a significant leap forward with their sophomore album, "The Entertainer." Released in October 2023, this album showcases the band's evolution and ambition, solidifying their position as a force to be reckoned with in the indie rock world[1].

"The Entertainer" is a conceptual journey that demonstrates No Such Animal's growth as artists. The album utilizes intros and interludes to create a cohesive listening experience, blending various musical styles from pop to prog rock. This approach marks a departure from their earlier work, presenting a more sweeping and grandiose vision while maintaining their signature sound[2].

One of the standout features of "The Entertainer" is its sonic diversity. The band has crafted an album that spans multiple subgenres of rock, creating a sound that is both familiar and refreshingly unique[3]. Tracks like "Witches in Salt Lake City" and "On My Own" showcase the band's knack for crafting catchy, energetic songs that resonate with listeners. The album's production quality is top-notch, allowing each instrument to shine while maintaining a raw, live-show energy that No Such Animal is known for[1][3].

The album's structure is particularly noteworthy. The first half is described as immensely fun and energetic, while the second half takes a more serious turn, adding gravitas to the music[1]. This shift in tone creates a dynamic listening experience that keeps the audience engaged throughout the album's runtime. The epic title track, "The Entertainer," is an eight-minute showcase of the band's musical prowess, blending grandiosity with vulnerability[2].

What sets No Such Animal apart is not just their musical ability, but also their genuine connection with their audience. The band's commitment to authenticity and meaningful engagement with fans shines through in their music and live performances. This connection is palpable in the album's lyrics, which touch on relatable themes and experiences, further cementing the band's appeal[3].

Overall, "The Entertainer" is a testament to No Such Animal's growth as artists and their potential to make a significant impact in the alternative rock scene. The album successfully blends nostalgia with innovation, creating a sound that is both familiar and exciting. With its well-crafted songs, conceptual depth, and undeniable energy, "The Entertainer" earns a solid 8.5 out of 10, marking No Such Animal as a band to watch in the coming years[1][2][3].
